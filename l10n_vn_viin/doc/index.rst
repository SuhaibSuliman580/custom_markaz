Installation
============

* Navigate to **Apps**.
* Search with keyword *l10n_vn_viin*.
* Press **Install**.

Instruction
===========

**Instruction video:** `Vietnam Chart of Accounts - Circular 200 and 133 <https://youtu.be/fiTzMvEbf_o>`_

**1. A Full Chart of Accounts.**

* Provide a full Chart of Accounts according to Circular No. 200 and Circular No. 133 based on the specified circular of your company.

.. image:: 01-he-thong-tai-khoan.en.jpg
   :alt: The Chart of Accounts
   :width: 1100
   :height: 500
   
**2. Add common taxes.**

.. image:: 02-bo-sung-thue.en.jpg
   :alt: Add common taxs
   :width: 1100
   :height: 450
   
**3. Add analytic tags.**

* Go to **Accounting > Configuration > Analytic tags**.

.. image:: 03-the-ke-toan-quan-tri.en.jpg
   :alt: Analytic tags
   :width: 1100
   :height: 500
   
**4. Add account tags.**

* Go to **Accounting > Configuration > Account tags**.

.. image:: 04-the-tai-khoan.en.jpg
   :alt: Account tags
   :width: 1100
   :height: 500
   
**5. Provide Payment Receipts in PDF according to Circular No. 200/2014/TT-BTC and Circular No. 133/2016/TT-BTC.**

* Template 01-TT: for receiving payments.
* Template 02-TT: for sending payments.
