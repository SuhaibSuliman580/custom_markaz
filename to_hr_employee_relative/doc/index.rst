Installation
============

* Navigate to **Apps**.
* Search with keyword *to_hr_employee_relative*.
* Press **Install**.

Instruction
===========

**Instruction video:** `Employee Relatives <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HYu_o6I-J74>`_

* To store employee's relative information, access: **Employees > Create a new one or access any employee profile > Private information > Enter employee's relative information**.

.. image:: nhap-thong-tin-nguoi-than.en.jpg
   :alt: Enter employee's relative information
   :width: 1100
   :height: 500

* Search whether an employee has a relative or not.

.. image:: tim-kiem-nhan-vien-co-nguoi-than.en.jpg
   :alt: Search whether an employee has a relative or not
   :width: 1100
   :height: 500

* On the Contacts app, users can use filters to find out who are relatives of employees in the company.
